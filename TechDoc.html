Technical capabilities of the Companions of the Hall
Drizzt Do'Urden

Drizzt is a Drow elf. His technical capabilities include:

  • Cutting things
  • Marrying Catti-brie
  • Befriending non-Drow
  • Brooding

Drizzt is additionally known for multi-classing as a Ranger, Fighter, and Monk. Though he has some magical capabilities, these are innate and he is not considered a spellcaster.

Catti-brie

Catti-brie grew up in Icewind Dale on the slopes of Kelvin's Cairn. She is a hardy and brave friend to Drizzt, and became his wife.

Although in her first life she was a fierce fighter, in her old age and in her second life she is known for her spellcasting abilities, which include:

Flame Strike
Healing Word
Polymorph
Change shape (a druidic ability, though not a spell)
Spiritual Weapon
Bruenor Battlehammer

Bruenor is Drizzt's best friend. He is also the king of Mithral Hall, and later rules Gauntlgrym. He is a hero among dwarvenkind, who know him to be honest, fair, and fierce.

Bruenor is also the adoptive father of both Catti-brie and Wulfgar, who are human orphans. Bruenor found Catti-brie in a ransacked village after her family was killed.

Wulfgar Son of Beornegar

At a towering 7 ft. tall, Wulfgar is a fine specimen of human athleticism. He was originally born to the Elk tribe of Icewind Dale. During a fight against the people of Ten Towns, he was captured and imprisoned by Bruenor, who later adopted him.

Of special note is Wulfgar's magical warhammer, which was made by Bruenor himself. The warhammer responds to Wulfgar's call, disappearing from wherever it is located and reappearing in his hand.

Regis

Regis is a halfling of ill-repute hailing from Calimshan. He is known for having several useful skills, and attained even more in his second life:

  • The ability to sneak stealthy among foes
  • The ability to eat as much as a man four times his size, or even more
  • The ability to climb like a spider
  • The ability to hold his breath underwater for more than an hour
Guenhwyvar

Guenhwyvar is a denizen of the astral plane taking the form of a great, 600-pound panther. She is Drizzt's long-time companion and confidant. She doesn't speak, but she is highly intelligent and understands Drizzt's commands.

Guenhwyvar comes to the material plane after being summoned from an onyx figuring of ancient origins. She can regenerate when she returns to her astral home.
